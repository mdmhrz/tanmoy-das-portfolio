About Me

I am Tanmoy Das, an Automobile Engineer with over a decade of experience in the automotive industry. I have worked with 3 renowned companies, contributing to innovative projects and solutions.

My expertise lies in automotive design, project management, and sustainable engineering practices. I am passionate about creating efficient and eco-friendly vehicles that drive the future of transportation.

I believe in continuous learning and innovation, always striving to push the boundaries of what’s possible in the automotive world.

I bring over 10.8 years of demonstrated expertise in automobile after-sales service and spare parts sales, gained through extensive experience in the automobile, farm machinery, and heavy equipment industries across Bangladesh. My career spans both local and multinational companies, where I have honed skills in automotive maintenance, troubleshooting, spare parts sales and marketing, client relationship management, team leadership, market analysis, and business development. With a proven track record of driving growth through effective team collaboration and strategic planning, I have successfully led initiatives to enhance sales and service operations in diverse automotive sectors.

Biography

Born and raised in Chaggoram, Bangladesh, I developed a passion for automobiles at a young age. Over the years, I have honed my skills and expertise in the field, working with top companies and delivering exceptional results.

My journey has been driven by a commitment to innovation and sustainability, ensuring that every project I undertake contributes to a greener future.

I am dedicated to mentoring young engineers and sharing my knowledge to inspire the next generation of automotive professionals.
